# Sign Qualities

## Elements

- **Fire**: direct, expressive, activating
- **Earth**: practical, concrete, stabilizing
- **Air**: conceptual, relational, communicative
- **Water**: emotional, intuitive, protective

## Modalities

- **Cardinal**: initiating, mobilizing, leading
- **Fixed**: sustaining, consolidating, resisting change
- **Mutable**: adapting, revising, distributing

## Reading rule

Interpret a placement as:

`planet role` + `sign tone` + `house area`

Example:
- Venus in Libra in House 7  
  → relating style + harmony/reciprocity tone + one-to-one partnerships

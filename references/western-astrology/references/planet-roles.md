# Planet Roles

Use these as concise semantic anchors during interpretation.

- **Sun**: core identity, will, vitality, direction
- **Moon**: emotional needs, instincts, safety, attachment
- **Mercury**: cognition, communication, learning, framing
- **Venus**: attraction, values, harmony, relating style
- **Mars**: drive, action, conflict, desire
- **Jupiter**: growth, beliefs, expansion, opportunity
- **Saturn**: structure, duty, pressure, maturation
- **Uranus**: disruption, freedom, originality, change
- **Neptune**: ideals, imagination, permeability, confusion
- **Pluto**: control, intensity, depth, transformation
- **Ascendant**: outer approach, persona, immediate style
- **Midheaven**: vocation, public direction, visible achievement
